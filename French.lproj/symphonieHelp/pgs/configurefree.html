symPhonie configuration for Free provider

symPhonie provides a very simple configuration for freephonie, SIP phone services offered by Free french provider.

note: the author is in no way related to Free.

There are two main steps : enable SIP services in Free configuration, and enter SIP informations in symPhonie configuration

To enable SIP account in Free management interface:

Login in free management interface

chose "Gestion de mon compte SIP" item

Chose and enter a password.

This password is different from your main Free password.
This password is the password you will enter in symPhonie configuration

If you wish to receive calls :

check the "Rediriger les appels entrants vers le SIP" item. You will then have the choice, in symPhonie, to redirect incoming call either to symPhonie or to your freebox.

When symPhonie is not launched, or when your MacBook is in sleep mode, calls will automatically be directed to your freebox.

check the "Activer le service" item, and click on "ENVOYER"

To configure symPhonie account:

open symPhonie preferences

select "Account" item

select "Free" as SIP provider

Enter your phone number ans SIP password

note: enter your Free phone number, not your line (France Telecom) number

note: enter your SIP password, not your main Free password

As soon as your phone number and password are correct, symPhonie will display the "offline" state.
